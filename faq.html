Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our honey products

How long does your honey last?

Our pure honey has an indefinite shelf life when properly stored. Keep it in a cool, dry place and ensure the container is tightly sealed. Natural crystallization may occur over time, which is a sign of quality and can be reversed by gently warming the honey.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services across Ghana. Delivery fees vary based on location. We typically deliver within 24-48 hours in Accra and 2-4 business days for other regions. Contact us for specific delivery information for your area.

Is your honey raw and unprocessed?

Yes, our honey is 100% raw and unprocessed. We only gently filter it to remove any bee wax or natural debris. This preserves all the natural enzymes, pollen, and beneficial properties of the honey.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash on delivery. Payment details will be provided during checkout.

Is your honey FDA approved?

Yes, all our honey products are FDA approved and regularly tested to ensure they meet the highest quality standards. We maintain strict quality control throughout our harvesting and packaging process.
