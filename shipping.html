Delivery & Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about our delivery service

Delivery Areas & Times

We currently deliver to all regions across Ghana with varying delivery times:

Accra & Tema (Same Day) GH₵ 15.00 - GH₵ 25.00
Greater Accra Region (24-48 hrs) GH₵ 20.00 - GH₵ 35.00
Regional Capitals (2-4 days) GH₵ 30.00 - GH₵ 50.00
Other Locations (3-5 days) GH₵ 40.00 - GH₵ 70.00

Delivery Process

1. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a WhatsApp message with your delivery details.

2. Our delivery partner will contact you before delivery.

3. Track your order status through our WhatsApp business account.

4. Delivery time starts from the moment your order is confirmed.

Note: Delivery times may vary based on location and weather conditions. We'll keep you updated on any changes to your delivery schedule.

Payment Options

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo)
  • Cash on Delivery (Accra & Tema only)
  • Bank Transfer
