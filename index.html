About Glo Honey

At Glo Honey, we're passionate about bees and the liquid gold they produce. Our honey is harvested using traditional methods that respect the bees and preserve all the natural goodness of raw honey.

Founded in 2015, our mission is to provide the purest honey while supporting sustainable beekeeping practices. All our honey is ethically sourced from our own apiaries and carefully selected partner beekeepers.
