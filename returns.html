Returns & Refunds Policy

Understanding our return and refund process

Return Policy

At Glo Honey, we stand behind the quality of our products. However, if you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Important: Due to the nature of our honey products and food safety regulations, we can only accept returns for:

  • Unopened products in their original packaging
  • Products with manufacturing defects
  • Damaged products during shipping
  • Wrong items received

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team within 24 hours of receiving your order
  2. Provide order details and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization and instructions
  4. Package the product securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item back or arrange for pickup (Accra & Tema only)

Please Note: Returns must be initiated within 24 hours of delivery. This helps ensure product quality and food safety standards.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund according to the following guidelines:

  • Full refund for damaged or incorrect items
  • Full refund for unopened items returned within 24 hours
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days
  • Refunds will be issued through the original payment method

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used honey products
  • Products without original packaging
  • Products returned after 24 hours of delivery
  • Special order or customized items
